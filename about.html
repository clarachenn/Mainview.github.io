Judy Zhu

Director

Judy graduated with a Master’s degree of Business & Administration (MBA) in Finance Management. She is a member of CPA Canada and has been a member of CPA China.

Judy has served as a financial manager in NEC group, one of the top 500 global corporations and used to work in Shanghai Wan Long Certified Public Accountants.

Judy has more than twenty years of professional experience in accounting, finance and taxation both in China and Canada. She is sophisticated in accounting system setup, tax planning and consultation, tax return filling, tax review and tax audit for individual, corporate, partnership and trust clients, dealing with various tax issues especially in tax area related to real estate investment, transaction, holding and assignment for both tax residents and non-residents.

Marilyn Chen

China area Manager

Marilyn graduated with a master’s degree major in Tax Consulting and Tax Planning, and bachelor's degree in Finance. She is a member of CPA China.

Has over 10 years of accounting and auditing experience, Marilyn used to work in KPMG as an external auditor and served as a corporate internal auditor in Beijing China.

Marilyn is experienced in tax declaration, financial audit in China; sophisticated in personal and corporate tax planning, corporate financial report audit and internal control audit, especially for those companies listed in mainland and HK China.

Her main practice currently in annual financial statement compiling and consolidated financial statement review of companies or affiliated companies listed in China, annual corporate income tax settlement audit and other special audits, such as internal control audit, outgoing audit, economic responsibility audit, single tax audit, etc.
